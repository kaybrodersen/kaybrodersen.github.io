Data science, causal inference, and the web

I'm a data scientist at Google, where I work on large-scale experiments, causal inference, and anomaly detection. An example of a project which I open-sourced is CausalImpact, a library for causal inference in time-series data (video). Before joining Google, I was a postdoc at ETH Zurich, Switzerland, where I worked on variational Bayes and high-dimensional time series (thesis).
