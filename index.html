Hi there,

I'm a data scientist at Google, where I work in our core analysis organisation for Search Ads. I'm passionate about product analysis, large-scale experiments, causal inference, anomaly detection, project strategy, and team development. An example of analysis software I've created is CausalImpact (video).

Before joining Google, I was a postdoc at ETH Zurich, Switzerland, where I worked on time-series analysis, neuromodeling, and variational Bayesian inference (thesis). This site lists my publications in these areas.
